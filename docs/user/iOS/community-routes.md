# Community Routes

The **Community** tab lets you browse routes shared by other OpenHiker users and contribute your own.

## Browsing Community Routes

The Community tab loads a curated index of routes from the OpenHikerRoutes repository.

### Searching and Filtering

- **Search bar** — Filter by route name, region, or author
- **Activity filter** — Show only Hiking, Cycling, Trail Running, or all
- **Near Me** — Toggle to show only routes within 100 km of your current location

### Route Information

Each route in the list shows:
- Activity type icon (hiking, cycling, etc.)
- Route name
- Author
- Region and country
- Distance and elevation gain
- Photo count and waypoint count

## Viewing a Community Route

Tap a route to see the full detail:

- **Map** with the track polyline and waypoints
- **Statistics** — activity type, distance, elevation, duration, region
- **Full description** from the author
- **Waypoints list** with notes

## Downloading for Offline Use

Tap **Download for Offline Use** to save the route:

- The route is added to your **Hikes** tab
- Waypoints are imported
- A confirmation message appears: "Saved for Offline Use"

You can then view the route detail, see it on your map, and reference it during your own hike.

## Sharing Your Routes

To share a hike with the community:

1. Go to the **Hikes** tab
2. Open a recorded hike
3. Tap the toolbar menu → **Share to Community**
4. Fill in the upload form:
   - **Description** — Tell other hikers about the route
   - **Country and area** — Where the route is located
   - **Author name** — Your display name
5. Submit

Your route is submitted as a contribution to the OpenHikerRoutes repository.

## Refreshing the Index

Tap the **refresh** button in the toolbar to reload the latest routes from the community.
